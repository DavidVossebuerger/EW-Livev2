Full EW Live System Workflow (End-to-End)
=======================================

Scope
-----
- Covers main.py entry, config loading, signal generation, ML probabilities, risk manager, execution, MT5 adapter, logging, storage.
- Audience: DevOps + quants; focus on control-flow and data-flow.

Abbreviations
-------------
- OM: OrderManager (execution.py)
- RM: RiskManager (risk_manager.py)
- SIG: Signal engine (signals.py)
- ML: MLProbabilityProvider (ml_model.py)
- CFG: LiveConfig (config.py)
- AD: MT5 adapter (mt5_adapter.py)
- EVT: event/loop cycle in main.py
- POS: open positions from broker
- MD: market data snapshot (price, atr, ema_fast/slow, volatility, spreads)
- HIST: historical outcomes (pnl, success, features)
